My Hobbies

I have had many hobbies growing up. My hobbies helped me relax and enjoy many aspects of life that I would have never been able to access without them. Some of them brought me a challenge, joy, or they were just a good past time.

One of my favorite hobbies is skiing. It brings the life back to me by exposing me to the fresh cold air of the snow covered mountains. As well, I have attained a level of proficiency in which I can try to specialize in a specific aspect of the sport. For me, I have decided I wanted to specialize in the Super G as I love to try to push the limits of speed I can attain on skis.

Another of my hobbies is sailing. Sadly, I can't do that in Colorado as most bodies of water aren't large enough. As well, during the winter, if the water freezes, I can't use it either.

As well, I enjoy other things such as video games, watching sports, hanging out with friends, and other leasurely activities.
